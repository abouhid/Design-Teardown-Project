PERSON OF THE WEEK

Lisi Linhart is a Creative Developer from Austria who is passionate about web animation. With a background in coding as well as design, she loves to combine technology with creative ideas — in the animation and UI experiments she shares on Codepen and the tutorials she publishes on her blog, for example. Lisi is also a lecturer at the University of Applied Sciences in Salzburg, where she teaches students about front-end development and design. You can find her on Twitter as @lisi_linhart. Thank you for sharing and caring, dear Lisi!
